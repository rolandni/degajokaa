It seems that the film was released a few days ago. Videos, stills of the film are still discussed on Facebook, YouTube. But the film ‘Munna Bhai MBBS’ has completed 20 years. The popular pair ‘Munna Bhai-Circuit’ of this film is still etched in the minds of the audience. Rajkumar Hirani’s film starred Sanjay Dutt as ‘Munna Bhai’ and Arshad Warsi as his assistant ‘Circuit’. These two stars shared an emotional post on the 20th anniversary of ‘Munna Bhai MBBS’ today.
20 years ago today, the film ‘Munna Bhai MBBS’ was released. The film did great business at the box office. That’s all, from nine to ninety, everyone’s heart was touched by the image of the prince. Sanjay’s sinking career was saved by the film ‘Munna Bhai MBBS’. Boman Irani, Jimmy Shergill, Gracie Singh were other main characters of this film. Sanjay Dutt’s father and late popular actor Sunil Dutt was seen in an important role in the film. He also played the role of Sanjay’s father in ‘Munna Bhai MBBS’.
On Tuesday, Sanjay Dutt posted a video on the 20th anniversary of the film ‘Munna Bhai MBBS’.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

He made the video covering several memorable scenes of this film. Sunil Dutt is also seen in this video. Sanjay wrote a heart touching caption with this post. The Bollywood star wrote, ‘Two decades of laughter, emotion, and lots of “jaadu ki zhappi”.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

A festive mood marking 20 years of \u201cMunna Bhai MBBS\u201d. A memorable trip. A few moments to never forget. I would like to thank everyone for this love, their love made this film timeless. Hopefully, “Munna Bhai 3” will be made soon.’
Meanwhile Arshad Warshi posted a beautiful post on the 20th anniversary of ‘Munna Bhai MBBS’. He posted a picture of the ‘Munna Bhai-Circuit’ duo and wrote, ‘The 20 years of “Munna Bhai MBBS” seems like yesterday. Thank you all for giving so much love to Munna and the circuit.’<\/p>\n\n\n\n

After the huge success of ‘Munna Bhai MBBS’, Rajkumar made its sequel. In 2006 he brought the film ‘Lage Raho Munna Bhai’. This film was built around Gandhism. The film ‘Lage Raho Munna Bhai’ was also a huge success. Again the ‘Munna Bhai-Circuit’ duo won the hearts of the audience. Then Rajkumar Hirani announced ‘Munna Bhai 3’. But there is still no update around this picture. Meanwhile, Rajkumar Hirani’s ‘Danki’ is going to release next Thursday.
